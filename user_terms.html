Live Matches (Events)

Live Final Result (LFR)

Prediction of the match outcome (1, X, 2) within the regular 90-minute duration.

  • 1: Home team wins
  • X: Draw
  • 2: Away team wins

Live First Half Result (LFHR)

Prediction of the outcome at the end of the first half of the match.

Next Team to Score (NTTS1,2,3,...)

Predict which team will score the next goal during the match.

  • 1: Home team scores
  • X: No more goals scored
  • 2: Away team scores

Note: An own goal is considered as a goal for the opposing team.

Next Corner Kick (NC1,2,3,...)

Predict which team will take the next corner kick.

  • 1: Home team takes the corner
  • X: No more corners awarded
  • 2: Away team takes the corner

Note: If a corner is awarded by the referee but not taken, it is considered void.

Next Team to Receive Card (NTC1,2,3...)

Predict which team will receive the next card.

  • 1: Home team receives the card
  • X: No more cards issued
  • 2: Away team receives the card

Note: Cards are valid only for players on the field during play. Cards issued during the break or after the match are not counted.

Live Over/Under Goals (O/U G 1.5, 2.5, 3.5,...)

Predict whether the total number of goals will be over or under the specified line at the time the bet is placed.

Live Over/Under Cards (O/UC L 3.5, 5.5, 7.5,...)

Predict whether the total number of cards will be over or under the specified number.

Live Over/Under Corners (O/UCN L 3.5, 5.5, 8.5,...)

Predict whether the total number of corners will be over or under the specified number.

Live Extra Time Result

Prediction of the outcome at the end of the 30-minute extra time period.

Over/Under Goals in Extra Time (O/U ETG 1.5, 2.5, 3.5,...)

Predict whether the number of goals scored in extra time will be over or under the specified line.

Live Tournament Winner (LTW)

Predict the winner of the tournament (the cup holder if applicable), usually for final matches.

Qualifies

Live Set Winner (SW1, 2, 3,...L)

Match Cancellation or Suspension

If a match is cancelled, suspended, or interrupted, the bet slip remains valid for 24 hours from the official start time.

Option: Free Kicks

A free kick is awarded when the referee stops play due to a foul or offside position.

Option: Shots on Target

Any attempt to score is counted as a shot on target if the ball goes into the net, is saved by the goalkeeper, or blocked by the last defender.

Formula 1 - ENGINES

Types of Bets

  • 1-RACE WINNER - DRIVER (RW): Predict which driver will win the race.
  • 2-RACE WINNER - TEAM (RW): Predict which team will win the race.
  • 3-PODIUM FINISH: Predict whether the selected driver will finish in one of the top three positions.
  • 4-HEAD-TO-HEAD (H2H): Predict which of the two listed drivers will finish ahead of the other.

In a 'Head-to-Head' bet, the winner is the driver who finishes higher in the standings. If both drivers do not finish the race, the one who completes more laps will be considered the winner. If both drivers complete the same number of laps, the bet will be void and settled with odds of 1.00. If one of the drivers does not start the race, the bet will also be void and settled with odds of 1.00.

  • 5-POLE POSITION (PP): Predict whether the driver will achieve pole position.

In the 'Pole Position' market, the winner is the driver who records the fastest time in official qualifying. Any later disqualification does not affect the bet result. If a driver does not participate in qualifying, the bet will be void and settled with odds of 1.00.

  • 6-RACE WINNER - TEAM (RW): Predict whether the driver will finish in one of the top six positions.
  • 7-CHAMPIONSHIP WINNER: Predict which driver will be declared the champion at the end of the season.
  • 8-CLASSIFIED / NOT CLASSIFIED: Predict whether the driver will be classified or not in the official race results.

A driver is considered classified if they complete at least 90% of the laps completed by the race winner.

Outright Bets
